About Silvassa

Silvassa, the capital of the Union Territory of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu, is a city known for its natural beauty, vibrant culture, and historical significance. Surrounded by lush greenery and serene landscapes, Silvassa offers a perfect blend of traditional charm and modern amenities.

Silvassa is renowned for its diverse attractions, including beautiful gardens, lakes, and cultural sites. Visitors can explore the rich tribal heritage of the region, enjoy various outdoor activities, and experience the tranquil ambiance that Silvassa has to offer.

Local Attractions

Silvassa is home to several attractions that showcase the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the region.

Lion Safari Wildlife Park

The Lion Safari Wildlife Park in Silvassa is a thrilling experience where visitors can see lions in their natural habitat. The park offers a guided tour and a chance to observe these majestic creatures up close.

Vasona Park

Vasona Park, located near Silvassa, is a beautiful park known for its lush greenery, scenic landscapes, and a variety of flora and fauna. It's an ideal spot for picnics, leisurely walks, and enjoying nature.

Our Lady of Piety Church

Our Lady of Piety Church is a historical church located in Silvassa. Built during the Portuguese era, the church features beautiful architecture and a peaceful atmosphere, making it a popular spot for visitors.

Hirwa Van Garden

Hirwa Van Garden is another beautiful garden in Silvassa, known for its lush greenery, fountains, and scenic landscapes. It's a perfect place for a leisurely stroll and enjoying nature.

Silvassa Tribal Museum

The Silvassa Tribal Museum is a must-visit destination in Silvassa. The museum exhibits the rich tribal culture and heritage of the local tribes, offering insights into their traditional lifestyle and crafts.

Location

Silvassa is situated in the Union Territory of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu. The city is easily accessible from Daman and other nearby cities, making it a convenient destination for visitors.

The best time to visit Silvassa is during the cooler months from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities and exploration.

Reaching Silvassa

Air

The nearest airport to Silvassa is Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport in Mumbai. From Mumbai, you can drive to Silvassa or take a domestic flight to the nearest airport in Surat.

Railways

Vapi Railway Station is the closest major railway station to Silvassa, located about 18 kilometers away. Regular train services connect Vapi to various cities, and from Vapi, you can reach Silvassa by taxi or bus.

Road

Silvassa is easily accessible by road from nearby cities. You can drive from Mumbai, Surat, or Ahmedabad, or take a bus to Silvassa and then use local transportation to explore the city.
