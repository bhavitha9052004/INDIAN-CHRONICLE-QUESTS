Tirathgarh Waterfalls

A breathtaking natural wonder nestled in Bastar, Chhattisgarh

Natural Splendor

Tirathgarh Waterfalls, located near Jagdalpur in Bastar, Chhattisgarh, is a magnificent cascade nestled amidst lush greenery. The waterfall plunges down from a height, creating a mesmerizing sight and a serene ambiance.

Surrounding Attractions

The area surrounding Tirathgarh Waterfalls is a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Visitors can explore the dense forests, go trekking, or simply immerse themselves in the tranquil atmosphere.

Accessibility

Tirathgarh Waterfalls is easily accessible by road from Jagdalpur, which is well-connected to major cities in Chhattisgarh. The journey to the falls is scenic, offering picturesque views of the countryside.
