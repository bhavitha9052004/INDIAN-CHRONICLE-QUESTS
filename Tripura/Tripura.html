About Tripura

Tripura, nestled amidst the verdant hills of North-East India, is renowned for its captivating natural beauty and profound cultural heritage. Once home to the illustrious Manikya dynasty, the state boasts a rich tapestry of archaeological marvels and a harmonious blend of tribal traditions with Bengali influences. Visitors to Tripura can indulge in vibrant religious festivals, admire the exquisite craftsmanship of cane and bamboo artisans, and embark on exhilarating treks through its picturesque landscapes. From the vivid hues of traditional attire to the tantalizing flavors of local cuisine and the bustling ambiance of its markets, Tripura offers a tranquil yet immersive escape, promising unforgettable cultural experiences and serene natural vistas that rejuvenate both body and spirit.
