Introduction to Bangaram Island

Bangaram Island, located in the Union Territory of Lakshadweep, is renowned for its pristine white sands, clear turquoise waters, and vibrant marine life. It is a popular destination for travelers seeking tranquility and natural beauty.

Attractions and Activities

Bangaram offers a range of activities including snorkeling, scuba diving, and sunbathing on its untouched beaches. The island is also known for its luxurious resorts and eco-friendly tourism initiatives.

Local Culture and Cuisine

The culture of Bangaram Island reflects the traditions of the local Maldivian community, with influences from Kerala and the Malabar Coast. Visitors can enjoy fresh seafood and traditional cuisine at beachside restaurants.

Conservation Efforts

Bangaram Island is committed to sustainable tourism practices, with efforts focused on preserving its natural environment and protecting endangered species such as sea turtles and coral reefs.
